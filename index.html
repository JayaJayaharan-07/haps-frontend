About HAPS

We Make Pure Water
Our Mission

Health Aqua Pure Systems (HAPS) is a leading water purification solutions company based in Tamil Nadu. We specialize in selling, installing, and servicing a wide range of water filter systems including RO, UV, and UF technologies from India's top brands.

Our certified technicians ensure every installation meets the highest standards, and our post-sales AMC services keep your system running optimally year-round.

ISI Certified All products BIS certified
Expert Technicians Trained service engineers
Fast Delivery Same-day installation
24/7 Support Always here for you

Mission

To provide every household and business with access to clean, safe, and pure drinking water through trusted technology and exceptional service.

Vision

To become Tamil Nadu's most trusted water purification partner, touching lives with healthier water every single day.

Complete Water Solutions
